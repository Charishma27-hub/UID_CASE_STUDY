Retirement Planning Solutions

At Nexus Bank, we offer comprehensive retirement planning solutions to help you build the future you envision. Our retirement specialists will work with you to create a personalized strategy based on your goals, timeline, and risk tolerance.

Retirement Account Options

Traditional IRA

  • Tax-deductible contributions
  • Tax-deferred growth
  • Required minimum distributions after age 72
  • 2023 contribution limit: $6,500 ($7,500 if 50+)

Roth IRA

  • After-tax contributions
  • Tax-free growth and withdrawals
  • No required minimum distributions
  • 2023 contribution limit: $6,500 ($7,500 if 50+)

Retirement Planning Services

  • 401(k) Rollovers: Consolidate your old retirement accounts for easier management
  • Retirement Income Planning: Create a sustainable withdrawal strategy
  • Social Security Optimization: Determine the best claiming strategy
  • Tax Planning: Minimize taxes in retirement

Our retirement calculators can help you estimate how much you need to save and project your potential retirement income based on different scenarios.

Education Savings Solutions

Invest in your child's future education with our tax-advantaged savings plans. We offer multiple options to help families prepare for education expenses from kindergarten through graduate school.

529 College Savings Plans

  • Tax-free growth: Earnings grow tax-free when used for qualified education expenses
  • Flexible contributions: No annual contribution limits (subject to gift tax rules)
  • Wide usage: Can be used at any accredited institution nationwide
  • Control: Account owner maintains control of the funds
  • State tax benefits: Many states offer tax deductions for contributions

Other Education Savings Options

Coverdell ESAs

  • $2,000 annual contribution limit
  • Can be used for K-12 and higher education
  • More investment options than 529 plans

Custodial Accounts (UTMA/UGMA)

  • Flexible use of funds (not limited to education)
  • Assets transfer to child at age of majority
  • Potential tax benefits for the donor

Our education savings calculator can help you estimate future education costs and determine how much you need to save each month to reach your goals.

Sustainable & Responsible Investing

Align your investments with your values through our Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) investment options. We believe you shouldn't have to choose between your principles and performance.

Our ESG Approach

  • Positive Screening: Invest in companies with strong ESG practices
  • Negative Screening: Exclude industries like fossil fuels, tobacco, or firearms
  • Thematic Investing: Focus on specific issues like clean energy or gender diversity
  • Impact Investing: Direct capital to measurable social/environmental benefits
  • Shareholder Advocacy: Engage with companies to improve practices
