About Nexus Bank

Who We Are

Founded in 1985, Nexus Bank has grown from a small community bank to a nationally recognized financial institution serving over 2 million customers. Our commitment to innovation and customer service has made us one of the most trusted names in banking.


With over 150 branches across the country and award-winning digital banking platforms, we make managing your finances simple, secure, and accessible wherever you are. Our team of professionals is dedicated to helping you achieve your financial goals.
