Manage Indian Earnings While Abroad

Our Non-Resident Ordinary (NRO) accounts help NRIs manage income earned in India like rent, dividends, or pension with ease and compliance.

Why Choose an NRO Account?

Indian Income Handling

Ideal for managing rental income, pension, dividends, or other earnings originating in India.

Joint Holding Options

NRO accounts can be held jointly with Indian residents or NRIs, providing flexible access to funds.

Tax Compliance

Interest earned is subject to TDS as per Indian tax laws, helping you stay compliant with regulations.

Seamless Transfers

Funds can be repatriated (up to $1 million per financial year) post tax compliance and documentation.

Who Can Open an NRO Account?

Any Indian citizen residing abroad (NRI or PIO) earning income in India can open an NRO account with valid ID and address proof.
