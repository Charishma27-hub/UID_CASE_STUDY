Bank Smart While You’re Abroad

Our Non-Resident External (NRE) accounts offer seamless, tax-free, and repatriable banking solutions for Indians working overseas.

What is an NRE Account?

Repatriable Funds

Funds in NRE accounts are fully repatriable — both principal and interest — in any currency of your choice.

Tax-Free Interest

Interest earned on NRE accounts is completely tax-free in India, making it a smart investment.

Joint Accounts

NRE accounts can be jointly held with another NRI, enabling easier financial management.

Internet & Mobile Banking

Enjoy full digital banking with secure access, anytime and anywhere in the world.

Who Can Open an NRE Account?

Any Indian citizen living and earning abroad (as defined under FEMA) can open an NRE account by providing valid passport, visa, and overseas address proof.
