Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to open an account?

To open any account with Nexus Bank, you'll need:

  • Proof of identity (PAN card, Aadhaar, passport, etc.)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, rental agreement, etc.)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • For current accounts: Business registration documents

Our digital onboarding process makes it quick and easy - you can complete most steps online!

Can I withdraw from my fixed deposit before maturity?

Yes, we understand that emergencies happen. You can withdraw your fixed deposit before maturity with these conditions:

  • Premature withdrawals may attract a penalty of 0.5-1% on the interest rate
  • The exact penalty depends on the remaining tenure
  • Alternatively, you can take a loan against your FD at attractive rates
